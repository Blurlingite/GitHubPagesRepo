About Me

Hello! I’m Vishnu, a game developer living in New York. I’ve always had an interest in creating games ever since I was a kid. Whenever the power would go out in my family’s house (which happened quite a few times) I’d make up boardgames or modify the ones we had so my siblings and I wouldn’t be bored. Now, I want to graduate to making video games for everyone to enjoy.

Here you will find the projects I have been working on. I absolutely love writing code for games and adding new features to them because it reminds me of what I am capable of creating. It’s the most satisfying feeling to come up with new additions to games (like adding a powerup, or introducing a new enemy for the player(s) to fight), put in so many hours, and eventually be able to implement them in a game. I can’t imagine myself doing anything else.
